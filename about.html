Modern Academic

I am a final-year IT student at the University of the Punjab, where I balance the logical world of technology with a deep-seated passion for fashion and aesthetics. At 21, I have cultivated a strong sense of personal style rooted in the "Modern Academic" aesthetic—a blend of classic sophistication and contemporary editorial edge.

For me, modeling is more than just standing in front of a camera; it is an extension of self-care and discipline. Whether it's maintaining my fitness at the gym or the strategic teamwork on the football pitch, I bring that same dedication to every shoot.

Based in Gujranwala, I am mobile and eager to collaborate with brands across Pakistan that are looking for a fresh, intellectual, and versatile face. I am passionate about storytelling through clothing and am ready to transform from a tech-driven student into the next face of high-fashion and commercial campaigns.
